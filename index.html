ABOUT ANGEL

Angel Martinez was raised in Aguascalientes (central Mexico) and later in San Diego, CA.
He studied acting at the University of California, San Diego. During this time he also did voice over work in numerous projects for his friend. Angel loves the creative process of bringing works to life to be enjoyed. He strives to bring justice to the material and the people/culture the work is based on.

Born in California. Raised in Aguascalientes (central Mexico) and later in San Diego. The film that sparked my love for acting, film and TV was Narnia (2005). I was in awe with the creation of a fantasy world to real life. This movie began my respect of film and TV as well as my drive to be in these worlds. Later I was introduced to theater with The Lion King on Broadway.

During my time in High school there was no theater program so the closest thing that I was part of was marching band. This depend my love for the harmonious interaction with story and the soundtrack/music. After High school I went to the University of California, San Diego to study Math and Computer Science. During my time at UCSD I reignited my drive for acting and began a Theater Minor where I took acting classes and started to work toward an acting career.
